## 1. SERVICES

The Service Provider agrees to deliver the following TikTok Shop management services:

### 1.1 LIVE Selling
- Host 2–4 hour LIVE selling sessions on the Client's TikTok Shop
- Professional hosts trained on the Client's products
- Custom scripts and content planning
- Scheduling and performance tracking

### 1.2 Creator Free Sample Program
- Recruit TikTok creators for product promotion
- Vet creators for quality, engagement rate, and audience fit
- Coordinate free sample shipments
- Manage creator relationships and content briefs

### 1.3 Discord Community Management
- Set up and manage a private Discord community for creators
- Share content briefs, campaign updates, and incentives
- Foster creator engagement and loyalty

### 1.4 GMV Max Ads Management
- Set up and optimize GMV Max ad campaigns
- Daily campaign monitoring and bid adjustments
- Budget management and ROAS tracking
- Weekly ad performance reporting

### 1.5 Shop Management
- Product listing optimization (titles, descriptions, images)
- Pricing strategy and promotional calendar
- Inventory monitoring and restock alerts
- Customer inquiry management (during LIVE sessions)

### 1.6 Reporting
- Weekly performance reports via email
- Key metrics: GMV, conversion rate, ROAS, creator performance
- Monthly strategy review calls

## 3. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 3.1 Initial Term
- **Minimum commitment:** 3 months from the Start Date
- **Auto-renewal:** Month-to-month after the initial term

### 3.2 Termination
- Either party may terminate with **30 days written notice** after the initial 3-month term
- Early termination during the initial term requires payment of remaining months' retainers

### 3.3 Performance Guarantee
- If the Client's TikTok Shop GMV does not grow by at least **20%** within the first 90 days of service (compared to the 90 days prior), the Client receives **one (1) additional month of service at no charge**
- This guarantee applies to the initial term only
- GMV measurement is based on TikTok Shop Seller Center data
