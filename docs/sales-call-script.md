# Sales Call Script — 15 Minutes

---

## BEFORE THE CALL (2 min prep)

Pull up in ARIA Dashboard:
- Lead card with their info (brand, category, GMV estimate)
- KaloData page for their shop

Have ready:
- Their TikTok Shop open in browser
- 2–3 specific observations about their current shop
- Your pricing (start at $2,500/month for first clients)

---

## MINUTE 0–1: WARM OPENER

```
"Hey [Name], thanks for jumping on! I know your time is valuable 
so I'll keep this tight — 15 minutes max. Sound good?"

[Wait for yes]

"Perfect. Before I share anything, I'd love to hear from you first."
```

---

## MINUTE 1–4: DISCOVERY (Let THEM talk)

```
"Tell me about your TikTok Shop — what's been going well, 
and what's been frustrating?"
```

**Listen for:**
- [ ] Current GMV (compare to KaloData estimate)
- [ ] Whether they do LIVE selling
- [ ] Number of creators they work with
- [ ] Biggest pain points (usually: time, creators, consistency)
- [ ] Whether they have an agency or team already

**Follow-up questions if they're brief:**
- "Are you currently running LIVE selling sessions?"
- "How are you handling your creator/affiliate program?"
- "Are you using GMV Max ads?"
- "What would you need to happen for this to be a big win for you?"

---

## MINUTE 4–7: DIAGNOSIS (Show expertise)

```
"Got it. So based on what you told me — and the research I did 
on your shop before this call — here's what I see:

1. [SPECIFIC OBSERVATION #1]
   Example: "Your top 3 products are averaging 4.8 stars with 
   200+ reviews, which is incredible — but you're only getting 
   about $[X]K/month in GMV. That tells me distribution is the 
   bottleneck, not product quality."

2. [SPECIFIC OBSERVATION #2]
   Example: "I noticed you have 12 creators in your affiliate 
   program but most of them haven't posted in 30+ days. That's 
   typical — creators need active management to stay engaged."

3. [OPPORTUNITY]
   Example: "Brands in your category that combine daily LIVE 
   selling with an active creator program typically do 3–5x their 
   current GMV within 90 days."
```

---

## MINUTE 7–10: SOLUTION (What you do)

```
"Here's exactly what we'd do for [Brand Name]:

1. LIVE Selling — We run 2–4 hour LIVE sessions with professional 
   hosts who know your products. We handle scripts, scheduling, 
   and performance tracking.

2. Creator Program — We actively recruit, vet, and manage creators 
   for your free sample program. We handle the DMs, shipping 
   coordination, and follow-ups.

3. GMV Max Ads — We set up and optimize your ad campaigns daily. 
   Target ROAS of 3x minimum.

4. Full Shop Management — Listing optimization, pricing strategy, 
   promotional calendar.

5. Weekly Reports — Every Monday you get a clear report with 
   GMV, LIVE stats, creator updates, and ad performance.

You don't have to lift a finger. We handle everything."
```

---

## MINUTE 10–12: PROOF / CREDIBILITY

**If you have case studies:**
```
"We helped [Brand X] go from $[X]K to $[Y]K/month in 60 days 
using this exact approach."
```

**If you don't have case studies yet (first clients):**
```
"I actually recorded a demo video showing exactly what a LIVE 
session would look like selling a product similar to yours — 
did you get a chance to watch it?

[If yes] Great — now imagine that running daily on your shop 
with a host who knows your products inside and out.

[If no] No worries — I'll send it after this call. But the 
point is: I've been deep in the TikTok Shop ecosystem and I 
know what works. I'm building this agency from the ground up 
and I'm being selective about who I work with. I'm taking on 
only 2–3 clients right now so I can give each one my full 
attention. That means [Brand Name] gets the A-team."
```

---

## MINUTE 12–14: THE CLOSE

```
"So here's how we work:

💰 Investment: $2,500/month — that covers everything I just described
📅 Minimum commitment: 3 months
🛡️ Performance guarantee: If we don't grow your GMV by 20% 
   in 90 days, you get a free extra month

We can start as soon as this week. I'd get access to your 
Seller Center, brief our LIVE host on your products, and we'd 
be doing our first session within 7 days.

What questions do you have?"
```

---

## MINUTE 14–15: HANDLE OBJECTIONS

### "That's too expensive"
```
"I get it. Let me put it in perspective — if we hit even a 
modest $30K/month in additional GMV, at your margins that's 
$[X]K in profit. The $2,500 pays for itself multiple times over. 
And the 20% growth guarantee means if we don't deliver, you 
get a free month."
```

### "I need to think about it"
```
"Totally understand. What specifically do you want to think about? 
Is it the price, the approach, or the timing?"

[Address their specific concern]

"How about this — I'll send you a quick summary of everything 
we discussed, and let's reconnect on [specific day]. Fair?"
```

### "I already have an agency"
```
"Great — what are they doing specifically? 

[Listen]

Most agencies focus on [X] but leave [Y] on the table. 
We could either complement what they're doing or — if you're 
not seeing results — replace them entirely. Either way, no 
pressure. Want me to do a free audit of your current setup?"
```

### "I'm not doing enough volume yet"
```
"That's exactly why this makes sense now. Brands that wait until 
they're 'big enough' miss the growth window. We help brands GO 
from low volume to high volume. That's the whole point."
```

---

## AFTER THE CALL

1. Log the call in ARIA → Outreach → Log (channel: Phone)
2. Update lead status (Meeting Set → Negotiating or Closed)
3. Send Post-Call Follow-up email within 1 hour (use template)
4. Set follow-up reminder for 2 days if they didn't close
5. Add notes to lead brief in ARIA
