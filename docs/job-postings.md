# Job Postings — TikTok Shop Live Selling Agency

---

## JOB 1: TikTok LIVE Selling Host

**Platform:** OnlineJobs.ph / Facebook Groups
**Type:** Full-time or Part-time (remote)
**Salary:** ₱15,000–25,000/month (depending on experience + performance bonus)

---

### Job Title: TikTok LIVE Selling Host

**About Us:**
We're a fast-growing TikTok Shop management agency helping US-based brands drive GMV through engaging LIVE selling sessions. We're looking for energetic, charismatic individuals who love performing on camera and can sell products through TikTok LIVE.

**What You'll Do:**
- Host 2–4 hour LIVE selling sessions on TikTok Shop (daily or 5x/week)
- Present and demonstrate products to viewers in an engaging, conversational way
- Interact with live chat — answer questions, thank buyers, create urgency
- Follow product scripts while maintaining a natural, authentic style
- Track and report LIVE session performance metrics
- Collaborate with the team on content planning and product selection

**Requirements:**
- Excellent verbal English communication (neutral or American accent preferred)
- Comfortable and confident on camera
- Previous experience in sales, hosting, streaming, or content creation
- Reliable high-speed internet connection (minimum 10 Mbps upload)
- Quiet, well-lit workspace
- Available during US hours (typically 8 PM – 12 AM PHT or similar)
- Own a smartphone with TikTok installed

**Nice to Have:**
- Experience with TikTok or TikTok Shop
- Experience selling beauty, health, fashion, or home products
- Basic video editing skills
- Ring light and professional microphone

**Compensation:**
- Base: ₱15,000–25,000/month
- Performance bonus: ₱500–₱2,000 per session based on GMV generated
- Equipment allowance after 3 months (₱5,000)
- Long-term growth potential as we scale

**How to Apply:**
Send a 60-second video of yourself presenting any product (your choice) as if you're doing a TikTok LIVE. Include:
1. Your name and location
2. A brief intro to the product
3. Why someone should buy it NOW
4. Your monthly salary expectation

**How to Apply:**

## Interview Question Bank

### For LIVE Sellers
1. "Sell me this pen in 60 seconds as if you're on TikTok LIVE."
2. "A viewer says 'this looks fake' in the chat. How do you handle it?"
3. "The product you're featuring goes out of stock mid-LIVE. What do you do?"
4. "What's your experience with TikTok? Do you have any content of your own?"
5. "How would you keep energy high in hour 3 of a 4-hour LIVE session?"

### For VAs / Creator Managers
1. "Walk me through how you'd recruit 20 creators in one week."
2. "A creator received a free sample 10 days ago but hasn't posted. What's your follow-up plan?"
3. "How would you organize and track 50+ creator relationships?"
4. "A creator posts a negative review of the product. How do you handle it?"
5. "Show me a sample outreach DM you'd send to recruit a creator."

### For Operations Managers
1. "You have 5 client accounts. Two clients didn't receive their weekly reports, and one LIVE host called in sick. Prioritize your next 2 hours."
2. "GMV dropped 30% for one client this week. Walk me through your investigation process."
3. "How do you handle a client who is consistently late providing product samples?"
4. "Describe how you'd onboard a new team member remotely."
5. "What tools and systems would you set up to manage operations for a remote team?"
