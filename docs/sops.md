# Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs)

---

## SOP 1: Daily LIVE Selling Session

### Pre-LIVE (30 min before)
1. Check product inventory — confirm all featured products are in stock
2. Review today's script — top 5 products to feature, pricing, promos
3. Test equipment — camera, lighting, mic, internet connection
4. Set up product display area
5. Post "Going LIVE in 30 minutes" on TikTok feed + stories
6. Pin promotional comment (discount code, bundle deal)

### During LIVE (2–4 hours)
1. Open with energy — greet viewers, state today's deals
2. Feature each product for 10–15 minutes
3. Demonstrate product use, benefits, comparisons
4. Engage with chat — answer questions in real time
5. Use urgency triggers ("only 5 left", "deal ends at the end of LIVE")
6. Call out buyers by name ("Thanks [name] for the purchase!")
7. Rotate through products, cycling back to top sellers
8. End with a recap of best deals and "follow for next LIVE" CTA

### Post-LIVE (15 min after)
1. Screenshot final LIVE stats (viewers, engagement, orders)
2. Log results in ARIA Dashboard → Revenue → Monthly Revenue
3. Note top-performing products and low performers
4. Send quick recap to client (if requested)
5. Save LIVE replay to TikTok profile

### Key Metrics to Track
| Metric | Good | Great | Excellent |
|--------|------|-------|-----------|
| Average viewers | 50+ | 200+ | 500+ |
| Engagement rate | 5% | 10% | 20%+ |
| Orders during LIVE | 10+ | 30+ | 100+ |
| GMV per session | $200+ | $1,000+ | $5,000+ |

## SOP 2: Creator Free Sample Program

### Step 1: Creator Identification
1. Search TikTok for creators in the client's niche
2. Filter by: 5K–500K followers, 3%+ engagement rate, US-based
3. Check their content quality and brand alignment
4. Record in ARIA Dashboard → Leads (use "creator" source)

### Step 2: Outreach
1. Use "Creator Recruitment DM" template in ARIA
2. Send to 20–30 creators per client per week
3. Track responses in Outreach Log

### Step 3: Vetting
- Reply within 24 hours of creator response
- Ask for their shipping address + content examples
- Confirm they'll post within 7 days of receiving product
- Set commission rate (15–25% standard)

### Step 4: Sample Shipping
- Request client to ship sample to creator
- Provide client with creator's address + expected content date
- Follow up with client if not shipped within 3 business days

### Step 5: Content Follow-up
- Day 3 after delivery: Check if creator received the product
- Day 7 after delivery: Follow up if no content posted
- Day 10: If still no content, flag as low-priority for future campaigns
- When content is posted: Share to Discord, thank the creator, track GMV

### Step 6: Ongoing Management
- Monthly creator performance review (sales, content quality)
- Top performers: Offer exclusive deals or recurring partnerships
- Low performers: Pause or remove from program
- Recruit 5–10 new creators per client per month

## SOP 3: GMV Max Ads Management

### Campaign Setup
1. Access client's TikTok Ads Manager
2. Create GMV Max campaign targeting the client's product catalog
3. Set daily budget: Start at $50/day, scale based on ROAS
4. Enable automatic targeting optimization
5. Set up conversion tracking

### Daily Monitoring (15 min/day)
1. Check spend vs budget
2. Review ROAS — target: 3x minimum
3. Pause underperforming ad groups (ROAS < 1.5x after 3 days)
4. Scale winning ad groups (increase budget 20% per day if ROAS > 4x)
5. Log performance in ARIA Dashboard

### Weekly Optimization
1. Review top-performing products → Feature more in LIVE selling
2. Review audience insights → Adjust targeting
3. Test new creative angles
4. Report to client with recommendations

### Scaling Rules
| Daily Spend | ROAS Target | Action |
|------------|-------------|--------|
| $50–$100 | 3x+ | Scale to $200/day |
| $200–$500 | 3x+ | Scale to $1,000/day |
| $500+ | 2.5x+ | Maintain, optimize |
| Any | Below 1.5x for 3 days | Pause and restructure |

## SOP 5: Daily Outreach Routine (Video Demo Strategy)

### ONE-TIME PREP: Build Your Demo Video Library

Before you start outreach, build a library of LIVE-selling demo videos:

1. **Identify your 5 target product categories** from KaloData:
   - Beauty / Skincare
   - Health Supplements
   - Home & Living
   - Fashion & Apparel
   - Pet Products

2. **For each category:**
   - Buy 1–2 similar products from Shopee PH or Lazada PH (₱200–500 each)
   - Set up professional recording area (ring light, clean background, good mic)
   - Record a **2–3 minute LIVE-style demo video** showcasing the product
   - Edit lightly — keep it natural, show personality, demonstrate enthusiasm
   - Save video + upload to Google Drive or TikTok (unlisted)

3. **Total cost:** ~₱1,500–3,000 for all 5 categories
4. **Reuse:** Each demo video works for 10–20 prospects in the same category

### Demo Video Script Framework
```
"Hey everyone! Welcome to the stream 🔥

Today I'm showing you [Product Name] — and let me tell you, 
this is one of those products that sells itself.

[Pick up product, show it to camera]

First thing you'll notice is [physical feature]. 
The quality is seriously impressive.

[Demonstrate usage — apply skincare, wear fashion item, etc.]

Now here's why this is a perfect TikTok Shop LIVE product:
• It's visually engaging — buyers can SEE the results
• The price point is right for impulse buys
• It photographs/videos beautifully

If you were selling this on your TikTok Shop LIVE, 
I'd run this session [time], push this with 
[number] creators, and target a [X]x ROAS on GMV Max.

This is exactly what we do for brands every single day."
```

### Morning Block (9 AM – 10 AM PHT): Prospecting
1. Open KaloData — filter for US sellers, $10K–$200K GMV
2. Identify 15–20 new prospects
3. Add each to ARIA Dashboard → Leads with:
   - Brand name, contact name, TikTok handle
   - KaloData GMV estimate (use `kaloDataGmv` field)
   - TikTok Shop URL (use `tiktokShopUrl` field)
   - Product category
4. Score each lead (revenue potential, brand fit, ease of closing)

### Outreach Block (10 AM – 12 PM PHT): Multi-Channel DMs
For each prospect, use this **priority sequence:**

| Priority | Channel | How | What to Send |
|----------|---------|-----|-------------|
| **1** | TikTok DM | DM the brand's public TikTok account | "🎥 Video Demo DM" template + attach demo video |
| **2** | Instagram DM | Find brand's IG, send DM | Same video + template (reformatted) |
| **3** | LinkedIn | Find owner/founder on LinkedIn | "💼 LinkedIn Outreach" template |
| **4** | Email | Find email from bio/website/LinkedIn | "📧 Cold Email" template + video link |

**Rules:**
- Start with TikTok DM (highest direct relevance)
- If no TikTok DM possible, try Instagram
- Always try LinkedIn in parallel (highest quality channel)
- Use email as backup if other channels aren't available
- **Attach your demo video** to at least one channel per prospect

### Follow-up Block (2 PM – 3 PM PHT)
1. Check for responses to yesterday's outreach
2. Log responses in ARIA → Outreach → update response status
3. Send Day 3 follow-ups to non-responders (use "Follow-up Day 3" template)
4. Send Day 7 breakup emails to non-responders (use "Breakup" template)
5. Book calls with interested prospects

### Evening Block (8 PM – 9 PM PHT = US morning)
1. Conduct sales calls (15 min each — use Sales Call Script)
2. Log call outcomes in ARIA
3. Send post-call follow-up emails within 1 hour
4. Update lead statuses in ARIA

### Daily Targets
| Activity | Minimum | Stretch |
|----------|---------|---------|
| New prospects added | 10 | 20 |
| Outreach messages sent | 15 | 25 |
| Channels used per prospect | 2 | 3 |
| Follow-ups sent | 5 | 10 |
| Calls conducted | 2 | 4 |

### Weekly Video Production
- **Every Sunday:** Record 1–2 new demo videos
- **Purpose:** Keep content fresh, expand categories
- **Budget:** ₱300–500/week on local products
- **Output:** 1–2 polished 2-min demo videos per week
